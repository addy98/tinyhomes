How to use Tiny Homes AR

Click the model viewer below to view an interactive AR model of a Tiny Home. Point your phone camera at an empty space on the floor to project the model. Once the house appears, walk forward in real life to explore the interior and exterior of the house. Move the phone from left to right to look around. To make the house larger or smaller, pinch the model with your fingers to zoom in or out.

To view the Tiny Home on your computer, simply click the link. Next, click the house to load the 3D model viewer. For a full-screen experience, click the bottom right-hand corner of the viewer. To explore, click and drag to look around the house and use your mouse to zoom in and out.

What is AR? AR stands for “augmented reality,” a type of technology that uses a device, such as a mobile phone, to project digital content onto the real world.

Learn more about how to use AR on your iPhone, iPad or Android here. * AR requires an iOS or iPadOS device with iOS 11 and an A9 processor or later. If it does not immediately appear on your mobile device, we suggest viewing it on your desktop or laptop.

Please keep in mind that it may take a few attempts for the model to load based on the device you are using.
